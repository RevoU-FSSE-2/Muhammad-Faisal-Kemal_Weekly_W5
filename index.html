About Us

SOKONGTANI connecting communities with capital and farmers in need of funding through agricultural commodity financing activities. This initiative aims to collectively enhance the scale of cultivation/agricultural practices and the well-being of individuals involved in the agricultural sector.

In our land, especially in countries like Indonesia, there are millions of hectares of land that have not been optimally utilized, and millions of farmers living below the poverty line. Meanwhile, the needs and demands of the community for food and agricultural products continue to increase every day.

On the other hand, research conducted by the United Nations indicates that agricultural land management needs to be doubled in the next 40 years to meet the food needs of the world's population. The FAO has predicted a 70% increase in global food demand, along with an additional 2.3 billion people by 2050. Therefore, increasing food production is both a challenge and an opportunity that can be optimized. One way to optimize this is by enhancing agricultural activities through funding and education for farmers.

SOKONGTANI is here to take advantage of these opportunities. With an experienced team, SOKONGTANI identifies the commodities needed in the market that have good characteristics and stable prices. Afterward, SOKONGTANI connects farmers in rural areas and available land, and opens up funding opportunities to urban communities.
